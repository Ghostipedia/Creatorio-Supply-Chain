## Hard recipes, while giving you the right tools at the right time!
Creatorio keeps most of the Andesite and Brass Age items available early on, so you can get to constructing your perfect factories on schedule!

However, it's not all that easy; you'll be establishing train lines or big farms to haul back exotic resources, and fuel your ever growing hunger for materials.

[## Custom tools and machines to assemble the greatest automation chains ever!]: #

## Multiblock Create Devices
Experience titanic contraptions built around custom multiblocks, with their own unique quirks and automation challenges to overcome, with plenty more on the way!

### The Crogiolo Forge
The first major multiblock you will build. Can you keep the Mother Foundry Blaze happy enough to produce your first Steel?<br><br>

## Questbook filled with lore, witty commentary, and plenty of love
Experience the major story chapters of Creatorio through every Canto (chapter headers), and find your way forward with every Intervallo (chapters themselves)!

**NOTE:** The questbook is currently under a constant state of development, and will experience changes. Please excuse the rough edges!
